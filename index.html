Marching Spotlight!

One way to get better at Marching Band is by watching other Marching Bands that possess the "next-level" skills that we are trying to obtain! Marching Spotlight features various spectacular High School Marching Bands, College Bands, DCI groups, and possibly even WGI performances!

Year: 2019

Show Name: Among the Stars

Group: Hebron High school

Location: Championships

Level: High School

Context: Hebron's 2019 show, "Among the Stars," combined familiar, emotional music like “A Million Dreams” and “The Impossible Dream” with complex, layered arrangements that showcased their musical excellence. The visual design used star-shaped formations, arcs, and radiating lines to reinforce the celestial theme. Color guard costumes and silks added a night-sky feel, tying the music and visuals together. The performance built an emotional arc of aspiration, struggle, and resolution, drawing the audience into the journey. Hebron earned 3rd place at BOA Grand Nationals and won Outstanding Music Performance, cementing the show as one of their most powerful productions.
